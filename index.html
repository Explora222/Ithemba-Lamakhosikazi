About Us

Empowering women, building communities, delivering excellence

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the construction industry in South Africa, Ithemba Lamakhosikazi (meaning "Hope of Women" in isiZulu) stands as a beacon of empowerment and professional excellence.

We believe in creating opportunities for women in a traditionally male-dominated field, proving that leadership, skill, and dedication know no gender boundaries.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class construction services while creating sustainable employment and leadership opportunities for women in the built environment.

Our Vision

To be South Africa's leading women-led construction company, recognized for quality, innovation, and social impact in every community we serve.
